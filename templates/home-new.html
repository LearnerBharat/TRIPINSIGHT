Top Places to Visit

Heritage

Udaipur, Rajasthan

Known as the "City of Lakes," Udaipur is famous for its palaces, lakes, and romantic ambiance. Visit the magnificent City Palace, take a boat ride on Lake Pichola, and explore the beautiful Jag Mandir.

Rajasthan Explore
Adventure

Leh Ladakh

A high-altitude desert known for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and adventure activities. Trek through the Markha Valley, visit Pangong Lake, and experience the unique Tibetan-influenced culture.

Ladakh Explore
Historical

Hampi, Karnataka

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring the ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire. Explore ancient temples, royal complexes, and boulder-strewn landscapes that create a surreal atmosphere.
